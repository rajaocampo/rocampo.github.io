Classic Pinoy Favourites

Sinigang

A flavorful and tangy Filipino soup known for its sour broth, typically made with tamarind, tomatoes, and other souring agents.

Menudo

A rich and savory Filipino stew made with diced pork, liver, potatoes, carrots, and bell peppers in a tomato-based sauce. It often includes raisins and hotdogs for added flavor and sweetness.

Adobo

Considered the national dish of the Philippines, Adobo is a savory dish made by braising meat in a sauce of vinegar and soy sauce. It is flavorful, tangy, and slightly salty.

Sisig

A popular Filipino dish made from chopped pork. It's often served sizzling on a hot plate, delivering a savory, spicy, and tangy flavor.
